# Landing Page Specification

This document defines a professional B2B SaaS landing page for an Amazon E-Commerce Analytics platform.

## Brand & Aesthetic
- Palette: Primary #3b82f6, Success #10b981, Warning #f59e0b, Neutral dark #0f172a, text #1e293b/#64748b
- Typography: Inter for body, Inter Bold for headings, JetBrains Mono for data
- Animations: Subtle fade/slide on scroll, number counters, hover lifts

## Structure

1. Hero
   - Headline: “Make Every Amazon Sourcing Decision Profitable”
   - Subhead: “Accurate EU profit margins—fees, VAT, break-even, AI recommendations.”
   - CTAs: “Try Free Calculator” (primary), “Watch 2-Min Demo” (secondary)
   - Animated dashboard snippet showing KPI counters

2. Pain Points (3 cards)
   - “Inaccurate Calculations”  
   - “EU VAT Complexity”  
   - “No Decision Insights”  

3. Solution Highlights (row of 5 large icons + text)
   - EU VAT Mastery
   - Input-VAT Reclaim
   - Break-Even & Scenario Planning
   - AI-Driven Recommendations
   - Portfolio Comparison

4. Interactive Demo Widget
   - Embedded form: category, price, cost, countries
   - Live KPI preview
   - “Get Full Analysis” button

5. Advanced Analytics Preview (3 mini-charts)
   - Pie: Cost & Profit Distribution
   - Line: Break-Even Analysis
   - Spark: ROI Trend

6. Recommendations Spotlight (3 cards)
   - Price-Test scenario with ROI impact
   - Packaging optimization ROI
   - PPC Budget headroom

7. Portfolio View Demo
   - Screenshot of bulk upload table
   - Callout: “Compare 500+ SKUs in under 1 minute”

8. Testimonials (2-3 quotes with star ratings)
   - Focus on VAT benefits, profit accuracy, decision impact

9. Competitor Comparison Table (7 features × 4 products)
   - EU VAT Accuracy, Input VAT Reclaim, AI Insights, Bulk Upload, Pricing

10. Pricing Plans (Free, Pro €29/mo, Enterprise €99/mo)
    - Feature lists, Yearly toggle, most popular badge

11. Final CTA & Footer
    - “Ready to Scale Profits? Try Free Calculator”
    - Link structure, social links, language selector

### Assets & Content
- Placeholder images: `/public/hero-dashboard.png`, `/public/demo-table.png`
- Use realistic demo data: electronics, books, baby food
- Testimonials: create new quotes emphasizing VAT precision and AI insights
- Pricing: use final €29/mo and €99/mo
